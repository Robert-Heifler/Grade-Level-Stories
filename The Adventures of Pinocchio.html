Geppetto carefully carved the wooden puppet with his sharp tools inside his small workshop. The old carpenter worked for many hours on the puppet's face, giving him a pair of round eyes and a funny nose that grew longer than normal noses. When Geppetto finished painting the puppet, he was surprised to see Pinocchio suddenly kick out at him from the table. The little wooden boy jumped down onto the floor and began running around the room with great speed. Geppetto chased after him between the chairs and tables, but Pinocchio was too quick for the old man. Before anyone could stop him, Pinocchio darted through the door and into the village street.
