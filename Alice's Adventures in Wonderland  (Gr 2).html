Alice was very tired from sitting next to her sister on the bank. She had nothing to do. She looked at the book her sister was reading, but it had no pictures in it. Alice thought what is the use of a book without pictures. It was a hot day and Alice felt very sleepy. She was thinking about making a chain of daisies when a white rabbit ran past her. The rabbit took a watch out of his pocket and said he was late. Alice had never seen a rabbit with a pocket watch before. She jumped up and ran after him across the field.
