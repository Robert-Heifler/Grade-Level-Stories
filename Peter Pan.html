Wendy and her brothers were fast asleep in their beds during the quiet night. The window between their beds was open to let in the cool spring air. Without any warning, a small boy dressed in leaves flew through the window and landed softly on the wooden floor. He was searching for his shadow, which he had lost during his last visit to their home. The strange boy looked under the beds and behind the curtains until he found it inside a drawer. When he tried to stick his shadow back on with soap, he began to cry from frustration. His sobbing woke Wendy, who sat up in her bed with great surprise at the sight before her.
