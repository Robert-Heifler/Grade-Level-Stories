Mary Lennox was a very sad and lonely girl. When her parents died, she had to go live with her uncle in a big house on the moor. The house had hundreds of rooms but most were locked. Mary had no one to play with and nothing to do. One day when it was raining, Mary found a key buried in the dirt. She wondered what door it might open. The next day she went outside and saw a robin bird. The robin seemed to be telling her to look at the wall covered in ivy. Mary pushed aside the green leaves and found a door. The key fit perfectly, and Mary found herself in a garden that had been locked up for ten years.
