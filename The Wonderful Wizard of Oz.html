Dorothy lived with Uncle Henry and Aunt Em in Kansas. Their house was small with only one room. The sun had baked the earth into a gray mass with little cracks. There were no trees, no gardens, just gray everywhere. Uncle Henry and Aunt Em never smiled. Dorothy had her little dog Toto and she played with him all day. Toto had long silky hair and small black eyes. He made Dorothy laugh, and she made him laugh too. One day a big storm came with a loud howl. The wind was so strong that the house started to shake. Dorothy sat on the floor and held Toto close.
